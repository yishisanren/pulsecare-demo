### 评估结果 · 臀腿疼痛（首次） / Assessment Result · Hip-Leg Pain (First)

---

### 分型文案 / Classification (Q2 描述词 × Q3 持续时间)

基于 Q2 疼痛描述词（5 类）× Q3 持续时间（3 时段），输出 15 条双语文案。
Based on Q2 pain descriptor (5 types) × Q3 duration (3 intervals), 15 bilingual outputs.

| Q2 类型 | <1 周 | 1–6 周 | >6 周 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Acute / Muscle-related**<br/>急性肌肉类<br/>(Cramping / Tightening / Spasm) | 产后早期肌肉紧张或痉挛，常见且多可自行缓解。<br/>Early postpartum muscle tension or spasm; common and often self-limiting. | 产后中期肌肉紧张持续，建议放松训练与姿势调整。<br/>Persistent muscle tension in the mid-postpartum period; relaxation exercises and postural adjustments recommended. | 慢性肌肉紧张，建议结合训练与专业评估。<br/>Chronic muscle tension; combine training with professional evaluation. |
| **Acute Nociceptive**<br/>急性伤害感受痛<br/>(Sharp / Stabbing / Shooting) | 产后早期急性伤害感受痛，常与局部组织损伤相关。<br/>Early postpartum acute nociceptive pain, often related to local tissue injury. | 急性伤害感受痛持续，需进一步评估组织愈合情况。<br/>Persistent acute nociceptive pain; further evaluation of tissue healing needed. | 慢性伤害感受痛，建议结合影像学或专科评估。<br/>Chronic nociceptive pain; imaging or specialist evaluation recommended. |
| **Chronic Nociceptive**<br/>慢性伤害感受痛<br/>(Aching / Dull / Heavy) | 产后早期慢性伤害感受痛，常见于长期姿势或负荷。<br/>Early postpartum chronic nociceptive pain, often from prolonged posture or load. | 慢性伤害感受痛持续，建议渐进训练与放松。<br/>Persistent chronic nociceptive pain; progressive training and relaxation recommended. | 长期慢性伤害感受痛，建议结合训练与专科评估。<br/>Long-term chronic nociceptive pain; combine training with specialist evaluation. |
| **Chronic Neuropathic**<br/>慢性神经病理痛<br/>(Burning / Tingling / Electric-like) | 产后早期神经痛，常与神经压迫或损伤相关。<br/>Early postpartum neuropathic pain, often related to nerve compression or injury. | 神经痛持续，建议进一步神经评估。<br/>Persistent neuropathic pain; further neurological evaluation recommended. | 慢性神经痛，建议结合专科治疗。<br/>Chronic neuropathic pain; combine with specialist treatment. |
| **Chronic Mixed**<br/>慢性混合型<br/>(≥2 description types) | 产后早期混合型疼痛，建议观察并记录变化。<br/>Early postpartum mixed pain; observe and record changes. | 混合型疼痛持续，建议综合训练与评估。<br/>Persistent mixed pain; comprehensive training and evaluation recommended. | 慢性混合型疼痛，建议多学科评估。<br/>Chronic mixed pain; multidisciplinary evaluation recommended. |

### 严重程度 + 训练频次 / Severity + Training Frequency (NRS)

| NRS | 严重程度 / Severity | 推荐模式 / Mode | 频次 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | 无症状，不训练 / No symptoms; no training needed | — | 无需 TENS / No TENS recommended |
| 1–3 | 轻度疼痛 / Mild pain | Mode 6 或 Mode 7（按类型）/ Mode 6 or Mode 7 (by type) | 3次/周 × 2周 |
| 4–6 | 中度疼痛 / Moderate pain | Mode 6 或 Mode 7（按类型）/ Mode 6 or Mode 7 (by type) | 5次/周 × 3周 |
| 7–10 | 重度疼痛 / Severe pain | Mode 6 或 Mode 7（按类型）+ 转诊建议 / Mode 6 or Mode 7 (by type) + referral recommended | 5次/周 × 4周 |

**Mode 命名 / Mode Names**：
- Mode 6 · 镇痛舒缓 / Chronic Pain Relief Mode（对应 Chronic 类型）
- Mode 7 · 止痛快速缓解 / Acute Relief Mode（对应 Acute 类型）
