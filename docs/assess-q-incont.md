## 3.2 尿失禁问卷

分为两阶段：3IQ 分型筛查 → ICIQ-SF 评分区间评分。共约16题。

### 阶段一：3IQ（3 Incontinence Questions）分型

| 题号 | 问题 | 选项 | 路由逻辑 |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Q1 | During the last 3 months, have you leaked urine (even a small amount)? | Yes / No | No → 跳过尿失禁模块 |
| Q2 | During the last 3 months, did you leak urine: (check all) | a) When coughing/sneezing/laughing/exercising; b) When urge/on way to toilet; c) Without activity or urge; d) With overflow | 多选 |
| Q3 | During the last 3 months, did you leak urine most often: | a) With physical activity (→ SUI); b) With urge (→ UUI); c) Without activity or urge (→ Other); d) About equally with both (→ MUI) | 单选，决定分型 |

**3IQ 分型结果映射：**

| Q3 答案 | 分型 | 推荐模式 |
|---------|------|----------|
| a (physical activity) | SUI（压力性） | Mode 8 |
| b (urge) | UUI（急迫性） | Mode 7 |
| c (no trigger) | Other | Mode 6 |
| d (both equally) | MUI（混合性） | Mode 8+7 交替 |

### 阶段二：ICIQ-SF 评分区间评分（Question 4-16）

| 题号 | 问题 | 选项 | 计分 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Q4 | How often do you leak urine? | Never(0) / Once a week(1) / 2-3x week(2) / Once a day(3) / Several times/day(4) / All the time(5) | 0-5 |
| Q5 | How much urine do you usually leak? | None(0) / Small(2) / Level 2(4) / Large(6) | 0-6 |
| Q6 | Overall, how much does leaking urine interfere with your everyday life? | 0-10 scale | 0-10 |
| Q7-Q16 | When does urine leak? (situational) | Multiple checkbox options | 不计分，用于临床参考 |

### ICIQ-SF 评分规则
- **总分** = Q4 + Q5 + Q6（范围 0-21）
- 评分区间：Mild(1-5), Level 2(6-12), Level 3(13-18), Level 4(19-21)

### 训练推荐映射表

| 分型 | 评分区间 | 推荐模式 | 频率 | 周期 |
|------|--------|----------|------|------|
| SUI | Mild | Mode 8 | 3x/week | 4 weeks |
| SUI | Level 2 | Mode 8 | 4x/week | 8 weeks |
| SUI | Level 3 | Mode 8 | 4x/week | 12 weeks |
| UUI | Mild | Mode 7 | 3x/week | 4 weeks |
| UUI | Level 2 | Mode 7 | 4x/week | 8 weeks |
| UUI | Level 3 | Mode 7 | 5x/week | 12 weeks |
| MUI | Any | Mode 8+7 alternate | 4x/week | 8-12 weeks |
