## 3.3 疼痛评估问卷

分为两阶段：红旗症状筛查 → NRS疼痛评分。共4题。

### 阶段一：Red-Flag Screening（Q1）

**Q1: Do you have any of the following along with the pain?**
- 多选，筛查需要医疗转诊的危险信号
- 选项：
  - Fever or unexplained weight loss
  - Numbness or tingling in legs / saddle area
  - Loss of bladder or bowel control (new onset)
  - Pain that wakes me from sleep
  - History of cancer
  - **None of the above**（与其他互斥）

**Red-Flag 逻辑：**
- 选中任何 red-flag 项 → 评估完成后结果页顶部显示医疗转诊提示
- "None of the above" → 继续正常评估

### 阶段二：Pain NRS（Q2-Q4）

| 题号 | 问题 | 选项 | 计分 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Q2 | Rate your average pelvic pain over the past week | 0-10 NRS slider | 0-10 |
| Q3 | Where is your pain located? | Multi-select: Lower abdomen / Lower back / Perineum / Hip / Thigh / Other | 不计分 |
| Q4 | How much does pain interfere with daily activities? | 0-10 scale | 用于 QoL 评估 |

### NRS 评分规则
- **总分** = Q2 单项（范围 0-10）
- 评分区间：None(0), Mild(1-3), Level 2(4-6), Level 3(7-10)

### 训练推荐映射表

| 评分区间 | Red-Flag | 推荐模式 | 频率 | 周期 |
|--------|----------|----------|------|------|
| None | No | 无需疼痛模式 | - | - |
| Mild | No | Mode 3 (Pain Soothe) | 2x/week | 4 weeks |
| Level 2 | No | Mode 3 (Pain Soothe) | 3x/week | 6 weeks |
| Level 3 | No | Mode 3 (Pain Soothe) | 3x/week | 8 weeks |
| Any | Yes | 显示医疗转诊建议，可选继续 | - | - |
